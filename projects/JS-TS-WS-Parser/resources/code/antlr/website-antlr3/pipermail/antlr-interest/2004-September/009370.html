There is one idea that works for c++ generator:

XXX {int set=0}:  
({(set & 1)==0}? TokenType1 {set |= 1;}
|{(set & (1<<1))==0}? TokenType2 {set |= (1<<1);}
|{(set & (1<<2))==0}? TokenType2 {set |= (1<<2);}
...
)+;
