> expr : ^(OR expr expr)
>      | ^(AND expr expr)

That's not equivalent; you want just

  expr : andExpr (^OR andExpr)*;

> The important thing here is that I want it to skip straight to andExpr in case there is no OR token.

The above syntax will do that.
