Question 1: can't I just fall-thru wrt to lexer rules, where lexer rules are specific-to-general, and avoid indeterminisms at run-time?

 ;

... // (EACH CONTROL CHARCTER HAS ITS OWN LEXER RULE)

    ;

    ;
