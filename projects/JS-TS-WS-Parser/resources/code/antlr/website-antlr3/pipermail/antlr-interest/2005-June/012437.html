A new day dawns and all becomes clear... Here is what I needed for
the definition of COLUMN:

*COLUMN : (~('#' | '\'' | '"' | '/' | '\t'| ' ') | ('/' ~('*' | ' ' | 
'\t'| '/' | '\n' | '\r' | '\'' | '#'))) (COLUMN_CHAR)+ ;

*I had to prohibit COLUMN from starting out like either a STRING or a 
COMMENT.
