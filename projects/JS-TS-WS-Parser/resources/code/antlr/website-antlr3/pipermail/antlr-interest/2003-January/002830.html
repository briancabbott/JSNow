antlr complains about not finding the EOF rule.

adding a final ? at the end seems to take care of things

SL_COMMENT
        :       "//"
                (~('\n'|'\r'))* ('\n'|'\r'('\n')?)?
                {$setType(Token.SKIP); newline();}
        ;
