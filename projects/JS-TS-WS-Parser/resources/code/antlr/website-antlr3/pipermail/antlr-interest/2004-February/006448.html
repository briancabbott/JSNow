Hi Chris,

I've just had a quick look and your nondeterminism may be avoided by 
changing the params rule:

params
:
	( SEMI ( WS )* param )+
	;


Note the change from * to +
Your original params rule could have matched nothing, which is 
usually to be avoided.

HTH,
Lubos.
