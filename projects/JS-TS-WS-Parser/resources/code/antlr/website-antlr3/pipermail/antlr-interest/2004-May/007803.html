>It does not have a newline.  Is there a work around for the
>single line comments?

Just add a question mark at the final newline match.

SL_COMMENT
: "//" (~('\n'|'\r'))* ('\n'|'\r'('\n')?)?
{$setType(Token.SKIP); newline();}
