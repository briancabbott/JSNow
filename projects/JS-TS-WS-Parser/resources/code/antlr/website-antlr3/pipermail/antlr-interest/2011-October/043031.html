> Hi Christian,
>
> Because you can't assign a label to an "OR-ed" group. You need to do
> something similar to this:
>
> class_modifier
>  : mod -> ^(CLASS_MODIFIER mod)
>  ;
>
> mod
>  : 'new'
>  | 'public'
>  | 'protected'
>  | 'internal'
>  | 'private'
>  | 'abstract'
>  | 'sealed'
>  | 'static'
>  | 'unsafe'
>  ;
>
> Or like this:
>
> foo
>  : class_modifier ID -> ^(FOO ID ^(CLASS_MODIFIER class_modifier))
>  ;
>  
> class_modifier
>  : 'new'
>  | 'public'
>  | 'protected'
>  | 'internal'
>  | 'private'
>  | 'abstract'
>  | 'sealed'
>  | 'static'
>  | 'unsafe'
>  ;
>
> Regards,

> Bart.
