Hi,

> Tomy,
> 
> I would try something like this:
> 
> In the Lexer:
> 
> INTEGER: ('0'..'9')+;
> 
> In the parser:
> 
> integer: INTEGER;
> double: INTEGER '.'  (INTEGER)?;

This allows whitespace around the '.' such as "1 .0" and "1. 0" and "1
.     0"

You really want the lexer to break your input alphabet into tokens.
