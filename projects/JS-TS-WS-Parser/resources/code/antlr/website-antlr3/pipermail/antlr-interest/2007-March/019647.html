Looks like ID_PART are still avoiding numbers. I removed the number
range from the ~ part. I dont know if its an already know problem. By
the way...

protected ID_PART :
        '_' | '$' |
        (
            {Character.isLetterOrDigit(LA(1))}?
            ~(
                '_' | '$' | '/' | '*' |
                '.' | '\'' | '\\' | '"' | '\t' | '\n' |
                '\r' | ' ' | '\f' | '(' | ')' |
                '{' | '}' | '[' | ']'| ';' | ',' | '=' |
                '+' | '~' | '&' | '<' | '>' | '-' | '!' |
                '^' | '%' | ':' | '?' | '|' | '@'
            )
        )
    ;
