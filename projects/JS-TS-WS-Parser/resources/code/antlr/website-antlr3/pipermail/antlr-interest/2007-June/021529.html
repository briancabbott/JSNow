Hi,

 

the problem is solved by putting a ^ before (A) or just by removing () from
(A)

 

grammar t;

k : ^(A)=>A |A;

A : 'a';

 

Or

 

grammar t;

k : A=>A |A;

A : 'a';

 

Hope it helps.
