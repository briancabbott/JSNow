Hi Daniel,

  Nope - the default Lexer for Group-Files is the AngleBracket-Lexer.

  use 
 	new StringTemplateGroup(<FileReader>,
typeof(DefaultTemplateLexer));
  or the appropriate Java-thingie ;)
