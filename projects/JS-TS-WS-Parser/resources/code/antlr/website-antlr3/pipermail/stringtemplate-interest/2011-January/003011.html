>

> ---------------
> <list:{x|<x>};separator=" AND ">
> ---------------
> 
> will produce the expected result:
> --------------
> A AND B AND C
> --------------
>

> ---------------
> <list:{x|<x>};separator= " AND ">
> ---------------
> 
> will produce this unexpected result:
> ---------------
> A" AND B" AND C
> ---------------
> 
> Adding more space will make it worse.
